About Berna's Kitchen

How it started

Berna's Kitchen started just right after the pandemic started. During that time, people aren't able to go out to eat the favorite food they want to eat. So, Berna's Kitchen was created.

With recipes dating back to the 1990s, the recipes Berna inherited and created from her mother's taste made your normal favorites into dishes with extraordinary tastes.

Every bite you will take, will surely make you forget your name because of how great it is!

Origin

Being Kapampangan of Origin Lea Buenaventura is culturaly inclined from childhood being inspired from the various cultural dishes that she was exposed to.

But her exposure towards the international cooking standard initiated and enhanced when she traveled to europe to pursure a job and in her time there she was amazed by the variety and means of cooking internationally and found out the extent at which her passion for culinary and food that she is able to reach.

Due to such inspiration and training she is now able to manifest a standard towards various Asian, Middle Eastern, American, and European quizine, while also manifesting a standard towards a workplace and at home environment.
